Bringing you the consumer insights and brand strategy answers you need.


The Cricket Group provides fullservice market research and brand strategy, across a wide-range of industries. We implement a custom tailored blend of creative and traditional qualitative, quantitative and secondary research to equip our clients with impactful insights and intelligence, including:

  • Consumer Lifestyles
  • Awareness, Attitudes, Usage
  • Journey Maps / Decision Trees
  • Consumer Segmentations
  • Product Research and Usage Testing
  • Customer Satisfaction
  • Advertisement Testing
  • Competitive Landscape Analysis
  • Pricing, Selection, Tradeoff Analysis
  • Website Experiences

    • Our Services

    We help you identify the idea/concept/project that resonates with your target consumer early in the design process to maximize your resources.
